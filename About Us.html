About MIAPSupport

MIAPSupport is a leading provider of IT software support for businesses of all sizes. Our team of experienced professionals offers a wide range of services to help our clients stay ahead of the curve in today's fast-paced digital world.

At MIAPSupport, we believe that technology should work for you, not against you. That's why we strive to provide our clients with personalized, cost-effective software support that meet their unique needs and challenges.

Our History

MIAPSupport was founded in 2023 by Wilfredo Hernandez, a seasoned IT professional with over 20 years of experience in the industry. Since then, we have grown into a full-service IT software support provider with a team of over 50 professionals.

Over the years, we have worked with hundreds of clients across a wide range of industries, from small startups to Fortune 500 companies. We are proud of the relationships we have built with our clients, and we are committed to delivering the highest level of service and support.

Our Team
